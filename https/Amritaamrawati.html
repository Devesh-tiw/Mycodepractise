Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's Amaravati campus is a growing, multi-disciplinary research-intensive institution located in Andhra Pradesh, accredited with 'A++' by NAAC and ranked among the top universities in India. It is one of the premier campuses of the Amrita group, offering engineering, business, and science programs.Key Details About Amrita Amaravati: Location: Kuragallu Village, Mangalagiri (M), Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. Rankings & Accreditation: The university is ranked 8th in India by NIRF 2025 and is NAAC 'A++' accredited. Programs Offered: The campus offers B.Tech (CSE, AI, etc.), MBA, M.Sc, and Ph.D. programs. Admissions: Primarily based on the Amrita Entrance Examination (AEEE) and JEE Main. Placements & Career: The 2025 B.Tech placement drive saw a high CTC of ₹80.4 LPA, with an average of ₹9.08 LPA. Companies like Google, Microsoft, and Cisco are among recruiters. Infrastructure: The campus is newly developed, featuring Wi-Fi, labs, and modern, well-maintained hostel facilities. Amrita connects students with industry-leading AI tools. Recent events have focused on Gemini-powered code on Cloud Run and Generative AI applications. The school of computing emphasizes AI research and training.
